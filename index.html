Save a Life

Not all heros wear capes, some wear leashes

Save a Life is a non profit foundation that provides support animals to those who need them but can not afford them.


Every donation is put forward to training and certifications of the animal.


Each person who applies goes through a screening process to determine if they are compatible with our standards.


We provide support animals of all kinds, from emotional to medical.


All who pass the screening, no matter the reason for applying are provided a fully certified animal.
